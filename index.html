AI Startup Journey: Potential Roadmap

A step-by-step guide to building the Horizontal AI Platform

Days 0-30

Investor & Ecosystem Connect

  • Reaching out and connecting with various potential stakeholders.
  • Initial due-diligence for partnerships and funding.
  • Focus on Venture Studios & Accelerators.

Days 30-60

New Company Formation & Strategy

  • Establishing the new startup entity.
  • Securing initial Angel/Pre-seed funding ($50K-$100K).
  • Idea refinement and identification of initial solution/product strategy.
  • Identification of Main Focus Vertical for MVP Stage.
  • Acquisition of majority stake in Kneogin Igmisarch Private Limited and turning it into a subsidiary for gaining access to developed IP till date.
  • Keeping burn to minimum from the Angel/Pre-seed round raised as initial starter capital.

Days 60-120

Programs, Team & Prototyping

  • Joining startup programs (Nvidia Inception, AWS Activate, Google Cloud, Intel Liftoff, Microsoft for Startups) for credits and resources.
  • Building and onboarding a small team (<10 members) for rapid prototyping and MVP development.
  • Joining a strategic incubator in India for enhanced support.

Days 120-150

MVP Launch & Go-to-Market

  • Launching the Minimum Viable Product (MVP).
  • Executing Go-to-Market (GTM) strategy for initial market validation.
  • Gaining initial traction and proving basic thesis.

Days 150-210

Seed Round & Acceleration

  • Raising a seed round to gain momentum and scale.
  • Potentially joining additional accelerator programs.
  • Focus on achieving product-market fit.

This roadmap outlines a dynamic path towards taking the first steps in empowering SMBs, Prosumers, Educators, & Students Globally with AI.
